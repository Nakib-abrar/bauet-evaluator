Auto-fill every BAUET faculty evaluation. Zero browser extension. One drag & one click.

Step 1 — Drag this to your bookmarks bar
★ Faculty Evaluator →
Click and hold, then drag onto your browser's bookmarks bar.

Step 2 — Use it

  1. Log into iems.bauet.ac.bd in your browser.
  2. Navigate to Faculty Evaluation → FacultyList (the page showing pending evaluations).
  3. Click the ★ Faculty Evaluator bookmark you dragged to your bar.
  4. A floating panel appears on the page. Pick your rating, type a comment, and click Start.
  5. The script opens a controlled BAUET window and submits every pending evaluation automatically. Watch the progress bar.
⚠ Your browser may ask to allow pop-ups for this site the first time — click Allow.

How does this work?

The bookmarklet opens a controlled iems.bauet.ac.bd popup and drives it from the parent window — clicking Start on each pending evaluation, filling every radio question with your chosen rating, writing your comment, and submitting. The original window stays in control across every page navigation, so you only click once.

All data stays in your browser. No servers. No accounts. No fees.

Don't want to use the bookmarklet?
